Our Journey

I was born and raised in a village where we used to eat fresh, pure chakki atta every day. The taste, aroma and quality were completely natural and healthy.

When I moved to the city, I realized that finding truly pure and fresh atta was very difficult. Most products lacked authenticity and freshness.

That is when I decided to start Healthy India Foods with a single mission — to bring back the same village purity and freshness to every Indian family.

Today, we proudly offer one focused product — Fresh Chakki Atta, made with honesty, quality and care.

Anant Chauhan

Founder, Healthy India Foods
